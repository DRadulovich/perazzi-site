# Service & Parts – Care Guides & Downloads

## I. Care Guides & Downloads *(new; editorial downloads)*
**Purpose:** Provide evergreen care guides (oil/grease points, storage, snap‑cap policy), downloadable as accessible PDFs.

**Fields:** `[{ id, title, summaryHtml, fileUrl, fileSize? }]`

**Interaction:** List of guides; each “Download” is an `<a>` with type/size indicated.

**Perf/A11y:** Links announce type/size; PDFs are tagged/accessible; `rel="noopener noreferrer"` and new‑tab behavior communicated in visually hidden text if used.
