# Service & Parts – FAQ (“Before You Ship”)

## J. FAQ — “Before You Ship” *(new; compact Q/A + optional JSON‑LD)*
**Purpose:** Answer shipping, packing, legal, and lead‑time questions calmly.

**Fields:** 4–6 Q/A pairs; optional policy page link.

**States & Interaction:** Static list; mobile may use collapsibles (Radix Collapsible).

**Motion/Perf/A11y:** None or minimal; reduced‑motion = instant. Questions as headings (`<h3>`), answers as paragraphs; collapsibles expose `aria-expanded`/`aria-controls`; links descriptive; strong contrast.

**JSON‑LD:** Only output **FAQPage** if genuine Q/A.
